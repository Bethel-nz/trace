In a land far, far away,
There lived a brave knight named Sir Author.
He had a trusty squire and a heart of gold.
Sir Author protected the kingdom from evil.
One day, a dragon appeared in the sky.
The dragon breathed fire and destruction.
Sir Author charged forward, his sword shining bright.
He fought the dragon with all his might.
The battle raged on, but in the end, Sir Author emerged victorious.
The kingdom celebrated his bravery.
